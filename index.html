10 COMMANDMENTS OF AAHM

1.Thou shall create rapport with clients before initiating care.
2. Thou shall have respect for all cultures, religions, races and genders.
3. Thou shall assess clients keeping in mind that culture, religion, race and gender have impact on health.
4. REMEMBER that treatment is NOT a one size fits ALL.
5. Thou shall NOT judge a “book” by its cover.
6. Thou shall know that a client’s pain is whatever he or she calls it.
7. Thou shall involve client in his or her care.
8. Thou shall respect clients wishes if not injurious to them or others.
9. Thou shall have good listening ears.
10.Thou shall maintain privacy and confidentiality.

By:Bosede Apata
